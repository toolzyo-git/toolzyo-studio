One-Time Session Access

Session access unlocks a single AI-generated output — the specific result you generated using your personal business information. Because this output is immediately delivered and personalized to you, all session-based purchases are final and non-refundable once payment is confirmed and access is granted.

Why no refunds apply here

Before purchasing, every user is shown approximately 30% of their personalized output as a free preview. By choosing to purchase, you have already seen a meaningful portion of your specific result and made an informed decision. This preview-before-payment model means the product has been partially delivered prior to any transaction.

The only exception: A full refund will be issued if a verified technical failure on our end prevents the output from being delivered to you. Refund requests under this exception must be submitted within 48 hours of purchase with a description of the technical issue.

  • No refunds once output is generated and accessible — regardless of whether you downloaded it

  • No refunds if you entered incorrect business information that affected the result

14-Day Tool Access
$127 · Single Tool · 14 Days

14-day access grants unlimited use of a single Toolzyo tool for a period of 14 calendar days from the time of purchase. A refund may be requested only if both of the following conditions are met simultaneously:

  • The refund request is submitted within 48 hours of the original purchase time

Once either condition is no longer met, the purchase is final

If more than 48 hours have passed since purchase, or if you have generated 3 or more outputs — whichever occurs first — the purchase becomes non-refundable. Both conditions must be true at the time of the refund request for it to be approved.

  • No refunds after 48 hours from purchase, regardless of tool usage
  • No refunds if 3 or more outputs have been generated, regardless of time elapsed
  • Access period begins at time of purchase and cannot be paused or extended
  • Unused days at the end of the 14-day period are not refundable or transferable
